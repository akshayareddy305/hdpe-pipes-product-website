Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of HDPE pipes? +
HDPE pipes are designed for water supply, gas distribution, sewage systems, and industrial applications due to their durability and chemical resistance.
What are the benefits of using HDPE pipes? +
They offer corrosion resistance, long service life, flexibility, leak-proof joints, and low maintenance cost.
How are HDPE pipes manufactured? +
HDPE pipes are manufactured through extrusion processes ensuring uniform thickness and strength.
Can HDPE pipes be customized? +
Yes, they can be customized in terms of diameter, pressure rating, and length according to project requirements.
